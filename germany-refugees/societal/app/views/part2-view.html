Now that you’ve examined the data and conducted ethnographic research, you should have a much better idea of the challenges the city faces. Work with your team to come up with some ideas to propose to the Mayor.

Your plan should have two parts: brainstorming and then prototyping. Show your best plan to help integrate the refugees into Erfurt in the short term, to help a smooth transition to longer-term resettlement. This is a big issue that calls for bold, even unorthodox ideas. Think of how to maximize your resources now, and how you’ll extend them into the future. Think of what kinds of big opportunities this could represent for Erfurt. This could be your big opportunity to reinvent your city, from a rust-belt former East German backwater into a model for change in a greater, global context. Let’s get to work.
