Now that you’ve conducted your ethnographic research, and heard the perspectives of various stakeholders, it’s time to do some data research. This is another important way to determine where the opportunities and possibilities are. We’ll look at a number of key metrics relating to population, society, education, commerce, and demographics to bolster our case.

The following two sources will get you started in learning more about the situation. You will need to find at least five or more other credible sources yourself to learn about the issues involved.
