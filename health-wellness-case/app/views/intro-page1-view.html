UNCGeneaology employees are generally young to middle-aged, college educated, tech-savvy, and unfit. They spend hours each day hunched over their workstations looking up historical records. Many eat at their workstations so there are lots of cans of air to blow away crumbs that get in the keyboards. Some employees have families and sometimes bring their kids into work. The UNCGeneaology offices are on two floors of a building in a very walkable part of downtown Greensboro away from traffic. There are plenty of restaurants and a few parks/green spaces within a few blocks.
